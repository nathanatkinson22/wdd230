Our story

Founded in 1964 when a brother and sister duo had a brilliant idea: Bring local produce grown in Southern California directly from the farmers to the people. After talking to a few farmers in the area, Bountiful Foods was born with their first road-side stand in Carldsbad, CA. Now with dozens of road-side stands, food trucks, and a brick and mortar restaurant, Bountiful Foods is a staple for fresh, locally sourced produce brought directly to you from the farmers who proudly call Southern California their home.

Carlsbad, California

Carlsbad, CA is a beautiful coastal city located in San Diego County, California. The history of the city dates back to the late 19th century, when a retired sea captain named John Frazier discovered a mineral spring in the area. Frazier decided to develop the land around the spring into a health resort, which he named after the famous European spa town of Karlsbad. The name was later changed to Carlsbad to reflect the American spelling.

In the early 20th century, the city became known for its agriculture, with farmers growing crops such as lima beans, avocados, and citrus. In the 1920s, the Carlsbad Irrigation District was established, which allowed for more efficient irrigation of the farmland. This led to an increase in crop production and a boom in the local economy. Today, Carlsbad is still home to many small family-owned farms that specialize in producing fresh fruits and vegetables, and the city is known for its vibrant farmers' markets and farm-to-table restaurants.
