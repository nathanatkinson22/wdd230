Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to build and strengthen relationships among business owners, entrepreneurs, community-members, and patrons in the Grafton, Utah community. The site provides information for business owners and community members. The site also provides opportunities to connect with other local business owners, meeting schedules, and other relevant information.
